Working Experience

After SPM, I worked for a year at a shrimp factory in Manjung, Perak. I once went two days without sleeping because our job is to prepare the shrimp for delivery to other factories located outside of Perak, such as Johor, Terengganu, Kedah, and Perlis. Our working hours are determined by the time requested by the customer. For example, if the customer from Johor requests that the package be delivered at 6 p.m., we must start working at 2 a.m. because the trip from Perak to Johor takes 10 hours. This experience has shown me that the real world is not what I expected.


Online Distance Learning (ODL)

Due to pandemic Covid-19 that surges in our country, we were already being told to switch to ODL which was operated through e-learning to keep maintain our social distancing. As a student, honestly, I’m also affected by the MCO through this ODL. At first, I was skeptical, as I have no experience in ODL before this. In addition, my internet access in my area is slow. I’m worried if it will affect my ODL process. However, things have gone smoothly and I started to get used to this ODL. I am very grateful to have lecturers that use learning methods that make it easy for students.
